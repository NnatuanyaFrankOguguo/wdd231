AC Technician Services - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: AC Technician Hub

Reason: This name was chosen because it represents a central hub where clients can find reliable AC repair and maintenance services, emphasizing professionalism and ease of access.

Optional Domain: ac-tech-hub.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a platform for clients to book AC repair and maintenance services, access troubleshooting guides, and learn more about our team and expertise. The website will offer detailed service information and an easy-to-use booking system.

Scenarios

  • How can I schedule an AC repair appointment online?
  • What maintenance tips can I use to improve my AC's performance?

Color Schema

  • Blue (#0074D9): Used for headers and accent elements.
  • Light Gray (#f4f4f4): Used as the background color for the page.
  • Dark Gray (#333333): Used for body text for easy readability.

Typography

  • Roboto: Used for headings (h1, h2, h3) to provide a modern and clean appearance.
  • Arial: Used for body text for a professional and legible design.
